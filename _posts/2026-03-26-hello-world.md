## Getting Started

You can now write posts in Markdown and they'll automatically be published to your blog. Each post should be named with the date and title, like `YYYY-MM-DD-title.md`.

## Features

This blog includes:

- **Dark mode, Light mode, and Sepia theme** - Toggle between themes
- **Archive page** - Browse all posts by year
- **Tags support** - Categorize your posts
- **Reading time** - Automatic reading time estimates
- **SEO optimized** - Sitemaps and metadata

Happy blogging!
